Wedding Gowns

The elements that create glamour are not specific styles - bias-cut gowns or lacquered furniture - but more general qualities: grace, mystery, transcendence. To the right audience, Halle Berry is more glamorous commanding the elements as Storm in the X-Men movies than she is walking the red carpet in a designer gown. Lace has been a staple in wedding fashion since the early Renaissance era, and its delicate and romantic nature makes it a timeless material. Its versatility makes it easily incorporated into any aspect of your wedding dress and can be as modest as you want.
